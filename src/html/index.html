About Me

I'm passionate about programming and making the interface that the user interacts with as easy to understand as possible.

I come from a field where social workers may or may not be tech-savvy, so I have a lot of experience in explaining technical concepts to non-technical individuals.

From my experience as a social worker, I've learned the importance of making things easy to understand for people with different backgrounds and abilities. This is especially crucial when they find themselves in stressful environments.

We've all experienced the feeling that things should just work or be easier, and that is what I'm hoping to achieve with my work.

In my free time, I enjoy powerlifting, hiking in the mountains, and exploring new challenges.
